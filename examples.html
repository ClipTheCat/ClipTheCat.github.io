Examples of individual cases

Beyond the statistics, there are numerous stories of bike helmets being the only barrier between life and death in serious accidents.

Celebrity chef Gordon Ramsay was in a bike accident in June of 2024. He has since made several posts advocating for helmets and attributing his helmet to saving his life, saying “Please, please, please, please wear a helmet. Because if I didn't, honestly, I wouldn't be here now.”

In March of 2025 in Eagan, Minnesota, a toddler was riding a scooter and was struck by a car. The three-year-old was wearing a bike helmet, which ultimately saved his life.

A woman in British Columbia was in an accident in 2018- while biking home from the grocery store, she was hit and run over by a pickup truck. Despite the truck running over her head, completely mangling her helmet, she left the experience with no head injuries at all.
